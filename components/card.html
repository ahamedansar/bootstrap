Spider Man

Spider-Man is a superhero from Marvel Comics. His real name is Peter Parker. He got his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He can climb walls, swing with webs, has super strength, and a “spider-sense” that warns him of danger. He fights villains like Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom, while also handling his normal life as a student and later as a young man. His famous lesson is: “With great power comes great responsibility.”
